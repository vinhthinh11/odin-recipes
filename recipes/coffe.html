How to make a cup of coffee for morning

Description

Perfect in the morning, as a midday pick-me-up, or alongside a sweet dessert, there's no bad time for a coffee break!

Ingredients

  • Filtered water
  • Correct proportion
  • The right grind

Steps

  1. Line the basket of your coffee
  2. Pour enough into the filter to wet it
  3. Measure the ground coffee into the wet filter
